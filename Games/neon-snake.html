üéÆ CONTROLS

Mobile: Swipe in any direction to turn the snake.

Desktop: Use WASD or Arrow Keys to change direction.

The snake moves continuously - you just control which way it turns!

üçé GAMEPLAY

Guide your snake to eat the glowing food that appears on the grid.

Each piece of food makes your snake grow longer and increases your score.

The longer you survive and the more you eat, the higher your score!

üíÄ ENEMIES & COMBAT

Green enemy spawns at 5s, purple at 15s. They hunt food too!

If enemies crash into your tail, they die and you earn 10 points per segment (but lose 1 segment, min length: 3).

Avoid crashing into enemy heads or tails - that's game over!
