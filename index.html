Bio

BOO is a psychedelic rock band formed in 2024 that loves experimenting across genres. After frontman Jeff lost his brother Kyle, who fronted Fields of Green, he discovered Kyle's unreleased music and picked up guitar to start the band.

Joe played with Kyle and took Jeff under his wing. Johnny drummed for Fields of Green and was close with Kyle, Jeff and the Tubbs family. Sean joined on bass, bringing experience from playing lead guitar for Andrew Judah and releasing multiple albums.

Music born from good times, loss, love and the desire to create something for others to enjoy.
